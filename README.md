Cadence

A minimalist workout companion for the gym, built entirely through AI-assisted development.

The Vision

Cadence fills a gap in the fitness app landscape. Most gym apps fall into two camps:
- Too casual: Planet Fitness-style "we're all in this together" vibes
- Too aggressive: Hyper-masculine "beast mode" aesthetics

Cadence takes a different approach: elevated, streamlined, and natural. No extreme tracking. No calorie counting. Just intelligent workouts when you need them.

Core Features

- Adaptive workout generation: Tell it you have 20 minutes at 6 AM, get a focused routine. Have 45 minutes? Get something different. Keeps training fresh.
- Audio cues: Seamless transitions between sets without staring at your phone
- Streamlined UI: Clean, distraction-free interface designed for the gym environment

The Build

This project started as an experiment: Could I vibe-code my way to a working iPhone app?

I've built mobile apps from scratch before (React Native, native Android) but wanted to see how far AI-assisted development could take a native iOS app.

Key Takeaway

Get your data layer right first. I reviewed and solidified the database architecture before building the UI. With the right foundation and planned extensibility, the rest comes together fast.

My background in software development and familiarity with Xcode definitely helped, but the AI-assisted workflow proved effective for rapid iteration and implementation.

Tech Stack

- Native iOS (Swift/SwiftUI)
- Local data persistence
- Audio playback for workout cues
